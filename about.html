Our Story

       

A Tradition of Authentic Cuisine Since 2008

   
       
       
           
               
                   
                       

From Our Family to Yours

                       

Padria was born from a passion for sharing the rich, diverse flavors of our heritage. Founded in 2008, our mission has always been simple: to create delicious, authentic food that brings people together. We believe in the power of a good meal to create lasting memories.

                       

Every dish on our menu is a reflection of our family's recipes, passed down through generations. We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure the quality and taste that our community has come to love.

                   
                                   
               
                   

Our Commitment

                   

We are more than just a restaurant; we are a part of the Kirkland community. We are committed to providing a warm, welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to serving you for many years to come.
